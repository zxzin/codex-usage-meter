# Token Meter Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026

Token Meter does not require an account and does not collect, sell, or share personal data with the developer.

## Data processed by the app

Token Meter reads usage records from the Codex data folder selected on your Mac. It processes those records locally to calculate usage and animation speed. When Codex account credentials are already present on your device, Token Meter may request current usage limits directly from the Codex service so the meter can match the usage shown by Codex. The developer does not receive this traffic or its contents.

## Purchases

Mac App Store subscriptions are processed by Apple through StoreKit. Apple may process purchase and subscription information under Apple's privacy policy. Token Meter receives only the subscription entitlement needed to unlock the app.

## Storage

Token Meter stores the selected folder permission and a local cache of the most recent usage limits on your Mac. Removing the app and its application data removes this local information.

## Analytics and advertising

Token Meter contains no advertising, third-party analytics, or developer-operated tracking service.

## Contact

Privacy questions can be submitted through the [Token Meter GitHub repository](https://github.com/zxzin/codex-usage-meter/issues).
